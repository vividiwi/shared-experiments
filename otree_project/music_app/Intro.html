Experiment

This experiment investigates the relationship between music genres and memory. Specifically, it is built on two main sections. The first consists of a questionnaire, while the second involves a mnemonic exercise. In total, the experiment is expected to take approximately 5 minutes.

Silent Task

In this section, I will invite you to participate in the Silent Task. Put on your headphones and listen attentively to 30 seconds of silence, during which a list of 10 words will appear on the screen. Your task is to remember as many of these words as you can, without resorting to any external aids. Once the time has ended, the words will disappear and you will be asked a question.

Music Task

Next, I will invite you to engage in the Music Task. Put on your headphones again, as you will listen to 30 seconds of music, including pop, techno, or classical. Your task remains the same as in the Silent Task.

Perception Task

After completing the Silent Task and Music Task, I would like to know your perceived performance. Your feedback will provide valuable insights for my analysis.

Results

Finally, I will tell you whether your answers are right or not and I will calculate your total earnings.

Payoff Explanation

As a token of appreciation for your participation, you will receive 3 Euros. Moreover, you will receive 1.50 Euros as a bonus payment for each correct answer.
